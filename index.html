Frequently Asked Questions

Why does the app need my location?

Panchang timings like sunrise, muhurat, and Choghadiya are specific to your geographic location. The app uses approximate location on-device — it is never sent to any server.

Does the app work offline?

Yes. All Panchang calculations are performed entirely on your device. No internet connection is required.

Which languages are supported?

English and Hindi are currently supported.

How do I change the app icon?

Go to Settings within the app to choose from eight moon-phase icons.
