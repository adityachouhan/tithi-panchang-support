Privacy Policy

Tithi Panchang  ·  Last updated: April 7, 2026

🔒 Short version: Tithi Panchang does not collect, store, or share any personal data. Location is used only on your device to calculate Panchang timings and is never sent anywhere.

Information We Collect

Tithi Panchang requests access to your device's approximate location for one purpose only: to calculate accurate Panchang timings (sunrise, sunset, muhurat, Choghadiya) for your geographic area.

We do not collect any other personal information, including but not limited to: name, email, phone number, device identifiers, or usage data.

How Location Data Is Used

  • Used entirely on-device to perform astronomical calculations
  • Never stored on any server or database
  • Never transmitted to Aditya Chouhan or any third party
  • Never used for advertising, analytics, or tracking

Third-Party Services

Tithi Panchang does not integrate any third-party SDKs, analytics tools, advertising networks, or data services. All functionality is built natively using Apple frameworks.

Data Retention

Since no data is collected or transmitted, there is nothing to retain or delete. All calculations happen locally and are discarded when the app closes.

Children's Privacy

This app does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a revised date.
